Our SAT Improvement Philosophy

Our approach is all about helping students improve as effieciently as possible. Our system involves a unique diagnosgtic exam which tests students understanding of key concepts that are tested on the SAT, as well as their ability to apply their understanding when dealing with unfamiliar and tricky structure. Our diagnostic allows to create an optimized framework for your student's improvement based off of the concepts they don't currently understand that they are losing the most points as a result of. After our plan is created we give lessons on critical subjects as well as problem solving with students to ensure that they have gained an understanding.
